King Prawn Omelette

Description

The king prawns absolutely make this one and it tastes delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 6 king prawns
  • 60g beansprouts
  • 20g frozen peas
  • 1 teaspoon salt or to taste
  • A pinch of white pepper
  • 1 spring onion, sliced thinly

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and beat.
  2. Add beansprouts, peas, king prawn, salt and white pepper to the eggs. Mix together.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoon vegetable oil on highest heat in wok. Swirl oil around so it coats the pan.
  4. When oil is hot, add the egg mixture to the pan and keep moving the mixture around for 1 minute whilst it is still wet. As it starts to take shape, leave it.
  5. Cook for 2 minutes, then flip it, cook for a further 1 minute. Keep the omelette moving by shaking the pan so it slides around.
  6. Serve and garnish with sliced spring onions.
