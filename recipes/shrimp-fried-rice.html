Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

Shrimps and fried rice garnished with slices of spring onion!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 30g frozen peas
  • 150g shrimps
  • 250g long grain rice, cooked and cooled
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • spring onions, sliced

Steps

  1. Prepare rice. The rice is best cooked when it's cooled.
  2. Take the shrimps and squeeze them gently, squeeze any excess water.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok, on high heat until hot.
  4. Pour beaten eggs into wok, stir constantly. When the egg has cooked a little into a scramble, add peas and shrimps and stir for a minute.
  5. Next, add in the boiled rice into the wok and fry for three minutes.
  6. Add salt and soy sauce to taste and fry for another three minutes.
  7. Garnish with spring onion slices.
