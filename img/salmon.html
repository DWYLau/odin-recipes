Honey Garlic Salmon

Description

A perfect sweet and savoury 5-ingredient garlicky sauce with a hint of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to medium.
  2. Season salmon with salt, pepper and paprika.
  3. Heat the butter in a pan over medium-heat. Add the garlic and saute for about a minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the honey, water and soy sauce; allow the flavours to combine.
  5. Add in the lemon juice and stir well.
  6. Add the salmon steaks to the sauce and cook each fillet for 3-4 minutes or until golden.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
